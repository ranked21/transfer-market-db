Transfer Central

The top 5 European Leagues spent $455 million euros during the January transfer window alone. Numbers during the Summer transfer window are often even higher. As transfer fees have inflated over the last decade, management at top European leagues are seeking better financial clarity to better understand the market value of players so that they can better manage their money. Our app aims to fill that market demand by providing insight into player transfers to allow them to better estimate market value when buying and selling players.
